Free academic and career consultancy

Total Student Care (TSC) provides free higher education consultancy by experienced, professional and trained student counsellors. They will assess your academic, financial and professional profile taking your career aims into consideration, map your pathway towards your goals and provide the best advice in selecting the right course at right university at your budget.

Advice on documents requirements

After you make a decision on course, tuition fees, university and location, ,our team will advise you on the required academic documents, CV, Personal Statement, English tests and other supporting documents. We will help you with edition of CV and Personal Statement if required. We will also advise you on the correct English test for your chosen course. At our Dhaka, Bangladesh centre, we provide IELTS registration help and preparation classes.

Admission applications to universities

Upon receipt of sufficient documents, our university admission team members will submit your application, to your chosen university and follow up with admission officers for offer letter. Our team will keep you updated on the progress on your application, offer letter and advise you any pending documents. We will do our best to get the offer letter in time.

Help with scholarship application

To give you extra value, we will submit application to our partner universities and negotiate £500.00 to £5000.00 or more scholarship on tuition fees discount for you where possible. The amount will vary university to university; our team will advise you on the available amount before submitting your admission application and keep you informed on the grant amount awarded.

Interview preparation sessions

Most of the universities will take ‘academic credibility interview’ before or after the conditional offer as one of the conditions of the offer.Our expert and specialist team members will advise you on the potential questions and appropriate answers, provide you links and materials for preparation. To make sure that you are prepared for the interview, our team will give you preparation sessions till you are fully ready.

View Details
Help with unconditional offer letter

Universities will normally issue a conditional offer initially with few conditions. Our team will provide you spot on advice and supports so that you can fulfil the offer conditions comfortably and get unconditional offer in quick time. We will mediate between you and the university in meeting the conditions correctlyhelp you evading unnecessary delay and request CAS and visa letters.

View Details
Visa application advice and help

In association with our professional partners, our team will provide you advice on required documents and information for your visa application, prepare you for visa interview. The team would have, in fact, provided you advice visa application documents at the outset when you met us and decided to proceed with your admission. At this stage, the team will check all your documents and information for correctness and lodge error-free visa application in time.

View Details
Help with Administrative Review

In association with our professional partners, we provide ‘administrative review’ application services should students get visa refused. For students who did not apply via Total Student Care (TSC), our team will go through the visa refusal letter and provide you unbiased feedback on likelihood of success. For TSC’ direct students, we will always submit administrative review application unless student does not want to do it.

View Details
Pre and post arrival advice and supports

Total Student Care (TSC) team will help you with university tuition fees payment, opening student file with home country banks that are our partners, currency exchanges, document translation, finding accommodation, buying air ticket, meet and greet service at airport, assisting with university enrolment, UK bank account opening, registration with doctor. The team will provide on-going follow up services during your studies.

Earlier is better - we suggest that you start preparation for overseas university admission at least 6 months before the course start date if not earlier.
